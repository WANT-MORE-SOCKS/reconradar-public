üìä Scoring Rubric

The suitability score is calculated using a comprehensive evaluation across six key security dimensions:

The Six Dimensions:

  • Security: Vulnerability management and security practices
  • Compliance: Adherence to standards and regulations
  • Reputation: Vendor track record and market presence
  • Controls: Administrative and technical controls
  • Privacy: Data handling and privacy protections
  • Reliability: Service stability and incident history

Formula:

Final Score = min(Average of all 6 scores, Compliance Score)

This means your final suitability score is the average of all six dimensions, but it's capped by the Compliance score. This ensures that even if other areas score highly, poor compliance performance will appropriately limit the overall rating.

Why cap by Compliance? Compliance is considered a critical baseline requirement. A product cannot receive a high suitability score if it fails to meet essential regulatory and compliance standards, regardless of performance in other areas.
